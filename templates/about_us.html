About Online Upahar.

A gift-giving practice in a country with a developing economy like Nepal is still traditional and time taking. Though we want to give our loved ones and friends a gift, we can't do this due to missing solutions that help us quickly purchase and share an inheritance among them with ease. On the other side, there are varieties of brands and products hidden and need exposure to foster and grow in Nepal. We are also aware of how we are dependent on other countries for fulfilling our purchasing needs. Therefore, OnlineUpahar is a digital marketplace for promoting the production and growth, and sales of Nepalese products and brands. We have two aspects of offering on our marketplace, i.e., Nepal born Digital and Physical goods. Our ultimate goal is to help increase national production that would aid in the growth of the national economy and poverty alleviation among Nepal's mass population.
